# Interview Synthesis

## 1. Executive Summary

Short summary of the strongest findings and what they imply for the PO.

## 2. Key Insights

For each insight:

- Insight:
- Evidence:
- Confidence: High / Medium / Low
- Product implication:

## 3. Pain Points

Group pain points by theme. For each pain point:

- Pain point:
- Who experiences it:
- Severity signal:
- Frequency signal, if available:
- Evidence/quote:
- Current workaround:

## 4. Evidence And Quotes

Include short, relevant quotes or paraphrased evidence from the transcript. Tie each quote to an insight, pain point, or JTBD. Do not fabricate quotes.

## 5. Jobs To Be Done

Use this structure:

- Situation: When [context]
- Motivation: I want to [goal/progress]
- Desired outcome: So I can [outcome/value]
- Current blocker:
- Evidence:
- Opportunity:

## 6. Opportunities

For each opportunity:

- Opportunity:
- User value:
- Business/product value:
- Evidence strength:
- Risks or unknowns:
- Suggested validation:

## 7. Suggestions For PO

Provide practical next actions:

- What to validate next
- What to ask in the next interview
- What to clarify with stakeholders
- What not to conclude yet
- Potential backlog or PRD implications

## 8. Open Questions

List unresolved questions that need more evidence.
