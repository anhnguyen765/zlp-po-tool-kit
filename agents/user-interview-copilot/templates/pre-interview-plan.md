## 3. Research Questions

List the core research questions the PO needs answered. These are not always the exact interview questions; they are what the interview should reveal.

## 4. Interview Question List

Organize questions into sections:

- Warm-up and context
- Current behavior/workflow
- Pain points and constraints
- Workarounds and alternatives
- Decision criteria or adoption blockers
- Concept, solution, or usability probes when relevant
- Closing reflection

For each question, include:

- Question:
- Purpose:
- Suggested follow-up probes:
